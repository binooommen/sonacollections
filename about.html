About Sona Collections

Sona Collections Pvt Ltd. came into existence in 2014. We host a large variety of Ladies Salwar Kameez Suits, bridal lehenga, Sarees, kids wear, fashion jewellery & fashion accessories. Our extensive range of ethnic wear & fashion accessories are best suitable for wedding trousseau. Our garments boast of being extremely skin-friendly, easy to wash, color fastness and shrink resistant. Our designers closely supervise the garments stitched at our end. We host a number of Fashion Brands amongst a variety of materials such as pure cotton, pure georgettes, chiffon and pashmina. Owing to high quality and an attractive range in products, Sona Collections Pvt Ltd has become a global name, capturing market bases nationally as well as internationally. Being a quality oriented organization; we pride ourselves in offering the finest quality garments as per the set quality standards. We have a huge production capacity, which helps us in meeting the client's order in time.
